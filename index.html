Multi-Unit Consistency Made Easy

We help multi-unit operators scale their brand and operational standards by realizing the potential of their frontline

Why do the best multi-unit operators use Purview?

Dirve Consistency

Automated Check-Ins with your frontline give you 100% certainty that "things are the way they are supposed to be" and that you find out the same day when issues arise in the field.

Execute Faster

Rolling out a new initiative or change to your locations? Anyone on the corporate team can add a one-time question to the automated Check-In's, letting you see which stores are confirming the change is made and which have an issue.

Reduce Overhead

District and regional managers get instant visibility into the status of their locations, allowing them to prioritize store visits and confidently manage more locations than before.

More reasons to love Purview

Analytics

See which questions or categories are most problematic across different locations. See charts of compliance for specific locations vs your company’s average. Identify which standards are trending up or down.

Single Sign-On

Enable your managers and corporate team to sign into Purview using their Google GSuite or Microsoft Office 365 / Azure AD accounts.

Landing Page

Don’t have a communications channel at each location? Bookmark a landing page on a PC or tablet that always shows open Check Ins.

Start on a PC, Finish on a Phone

Employees can load a Check In on a store device and enter in their cellphone number to get an SMS link to do the Check In from their phone.

E-mail and Slack Notifications

Have a comms channel at each location? We’ll send a notification whenever a Check In is open or, later, about to close.

Custom Scheduling

Set questions to only appear on certain days of the week. Edit schedules to vary for specific locations or specific days.

E-mail Reports

Location, district, and regional managers can select exactly the type of e-mails they want to highlight new issues, see new images, and more.

Webhooks

No Accounts Needed for Frontline Workers
